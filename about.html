Inspired by Team Curio:
A Startup Weekend project
A team of winners!
In Amsterdam at the super-fantastic mega-massive extraordinary and unforgetable event called Startup Weekend, an epic team of mega-talented super-heros was formed to fix the future of online learning...forever, voila! In this special edition of Amsterdam Startup Weekend (2016), the theme was Education Technology, so you can imagine only the best and the brightest were involved.


Team Curio was comprised of a perfect mix of professional engineers, marketers, designers, leadership, and online learners. After fifty-four straight hours of on-going brainstorms, research, design, and validation, our incredible team came up with an idea worthy of investment. Sadly at that time every memebers of the very dynamica team already held full-time commitments and could not afford to focus much on Team Curio. On the bright side, nothing can stop one of the greatest teams ever from catching up for a little fun from time to time.


In an obvious attempt to revitalize the squadron and inspire original members to quit there day-jobs, the team's front-end designer (Alex Wyrick) thought it couldn't hurt to build this landing page as a simple reminder of the magic they once shared ;)
